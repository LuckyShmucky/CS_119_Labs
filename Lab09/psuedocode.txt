# input 
create a form that gets the number of miles driven, number of gallons 
put into gas tank, and cost in dollars per gallon of gas

# process
to find total fuel cost: number of gallons put into gas tank multiplied 
by cost per gallon of gas

to find MPG divide the number of miles driven by the amount of gallons put
into the gas tank

to find price per mile: divide total fuel cost by number of miles driven


# output
print or return MPG, total fuel cost, and cost per mile
